About us

AMIX Int. Co. Ltd is the group of companies registered in SRV, PRC, RF.
Headquartered in Hanoi
Our mission is to support our partners and clients in the development of international business relations. Different jurisdictions allow us to be flexible and bypass the obstacles caused by geopolitical changes. As a result of our work, companies enter new markets, find reliable manufacturers and suppliers from the regions of Southeast Asia and the CIS. We have up-to-date information on investment attractiveness in the areas of trade, production, real estate in the territories of our presence.
We providing best servicies for your business expansion without intermediaries.
